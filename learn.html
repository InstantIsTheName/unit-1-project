What is Health Insurance?
Health Insurance help to cover all or some medical costs for fixed monthly amount.

Why is affortable Health Insurance important?
In America medical expenses are the second leading cause of bankruptcies. So it is important that we find plans that will help alleviate some of the financial hardships that come along with having either overcharged insurance or none at all. Without the right coverage you can be paying lots of money out of pocket causing you to make sacrifices for treatments and basic care. This can be especially harmful for people with disability or elderly who rely heavily on treatment and medical devices to move through the day or to live.
